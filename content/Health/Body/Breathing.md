## Breathing
book to read: Breath: The New Science of a Lost Art by James Nestor

1. No mouth breathing
2. Only nose breathing
3. Exhale slower than inhale
4. Breathe less
5. 5.5 times per minute
6. Not processed food, chewing
7. Exercises: 
    * numbers on exhale
    * change nostrils
    * mewing
